Our Brand Identity

We are Trustworthy

The TeamBond team believes working in a psychologically safe environment fosters growth and innovation. When team members feel safe and trust their colleagues and partners, they are more open to be collaborating and working together towards a common goal.

We are Empathetic

We understand and are empathetic. Our team faced similar problems in the workplace and understand what is needed to solve complex problems.

We believe in Innovation and Creativity

We believe that continuous improvement is essential in a service-oriented platform. After all, everything around us is always changing. Working in a positive environment fosters innovation and creativity so that we can continually improve our offering.

We believe in Grit

We were faced with many obstacles when we initially started working on TeamBond. But we believed that we had something special to offer and that with hard work and perseverance, we could really make a difference.

We value Humility in ourselves and our peers

The best teams are about a group of people coming together to solve a problem. Without each team member, the same solution would not have been reached. Humility drives us to focus on the end solution to continuously improve together.

Message from Leila

With a career spanning over two decades in staffing and consulting, I evaluated and provided talented workers to some of the largest companies in the world.
Much to the chagrin of my previous managers, it wasn't about the quick buck or the commission for me. My main focus was building long-term relationships. I wanted to work with like-minded professionals to help them solve their most complex problems. My theory was if you had strong relationships, business would follow. I was right that business would follow. But I was still focusing on placing individuals onto teams hoping they would fit in with the team after they were placed.
Clients and colleagues all faced the same issue - where can they build and/or find a top-performing team to solve their problems? The staffing, consulting and managed services are billion dollar industries that profit by providing talent to work at companies. The focus has always been on the individual worker to fill a vacant role on a team, not taking into consideration if he/she would be a good addition to the team. Consulting and managed services companies try to focus on teams, but still place individuals onto teams, hoping for the best outcome where the team works well together. These created teams often resort to the 80/20 rule, where 20 percent of the team do all of the work and carry the weight of the entire team, which ultimately does not solve the problem of hiring a top-performing team.
TeamBond set out to solve this problem and change how companies hire talent and more importantly, where they can hire teams.
Good teamwork is essential in all organizations. It isn't a secret that the best performing teams work well together and have a synergy - where the combined effect of the team is greater than the sum of the individual efforts. Organizations are much more likely to perform well when their people work effectively as a team. TeamBond solves the problem of who is right for the team by considering behavioral science in addition to a worker's skills, education and experience. TeamBond is revolutionizing the staffing and recruitment process, making it easier and quicker for companies to build teams.
TeamBond will also bring the best jobs to workers. Only jobs on teams that a worker is a fit for will be recommended to the worker, thus streamlining and changing how you find your next role.
We invite you to experience TeamBond, an online platform that creates Teams by Design.
